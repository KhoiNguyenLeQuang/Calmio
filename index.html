You deserve support right now

If you are thinking about suicide or hurting yourself, or you're worried about a friend, you can talk to someone immediately - free and confidential. You don't need to be in crisis to call.

Tapping a button dials from your device - this website can't place a call for you. Administrators can edit these numbers in School settings.

Our privacy policy

What Calmio keeps. Your username, email address, real name, school, profile, messages to the counseling team, appreciation notes, booked sessions, and your garden. Nothing more.

How it is protected. Everything is encrypted before it is stored, and your password is never saved in a readable form. Sign-in pauses after repeated wrong guesses, and Calmio locks itself when you step away. Closing the page signs you out completely.

Who can see what. Thoughts you share go only to the school's counselors. If you send a message anonymously, counselors only ever see a code name - never who you are. Your photo and nickname are shown publicly only if you clearly say yes when leaving Calmio.

What Calmio never does. It never sells data, never shares it outside your school's counseling team, and never sends your information to any other website.

Your choices. You can delete your account at any time from the account manager; this permanently removes your profile, messages, sessions, and garden.

For parents and the school. Calmio is run by the school for its students. Because most users are minors, the school informs parents or guardians about the service and how their child's information is handled, in line with Vietnam's personal data protection rules. Questions go to the school administrator.

This is a draft policy. The school must review and adapt it (ideally with legal advice) before real use.
